The Spiritual Journey

Welcome to the digital archive of the Vishnu Sahasranama discourse by Shri. Ramabhadran V Guruji. This portal is designed to provide a structured path through the daily sessions, exploring the linguistic precision, spiritual depth, and philosophical frameworks of the Thousand Names.

How to navigate:

Use the sidebar to the left (or the top slider on mobile) to select a discourse day. Each page is equipped with the video session, segmented notes, and technical references for the ślokas covered.
