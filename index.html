The Vedic Path: From Ancient Theory to the Sahasranāmam

The Three Guṇas

The discourse begins with the three guṇas: Sattvaguṇa (Calm), Rajoguṇa (Restless), and Tamoguṇa (Lethargic).

Role of the Guru

A Guru is essential to interpret mantras like the Śānti Mantra, explaining how Bhagavān remains complete.

Itihāsas as Practical Proof

The ṛṣis composed Purāṇas and Itihāsas as practical demonstrations of Vedic theory.
