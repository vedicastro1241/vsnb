Day 5: The Divine Vision (Dhyānam)

The Universe as Mahāviṣṇu

Day 5 introduces the Viśvarūpa. All beings reside within Him. His feet are the earth, His navel is the sky, and His eyes are the sun and moon.

AttributeCosmic Manifestation
FeetThe Earth (Bhūmi)
EyesSun and Moon

Śāntākāraṃ Analysis

Meghavarṇaṃ: Dark like rain clouds, full of compassion (karuṇā) to shower on us.

Viśvadhāraṃ: The material support of the universe, like gold to jewelry.

The Mark of Compassion

The Śrīvatsa mark on the chest commemorates Bhṛgu Maharṣi's test, proving Viṣṇu is the most sāttvika deity through His supreme patience.

Kṛṣṇa & Satyabhāmā

Kṛṣṇa sits on the Siṃhāsana under the Pārijāta tree. We approach Him through Mahālakṣmī, the Mother, who mediates for our sins.

Practical Remedies

  • Financial Debt: Chant Śloka 65 10 times daily.
  • Spiritual Growth: Practice Śravaṇam, Mananam, and Nididhyāsanam.
