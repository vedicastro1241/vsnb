Day 5: The Divine Vision (Dhyānam)

The Universe as Mahāviṣṇu

His feet are the earth, His eyes are the sun and moon. All beings reside within Him.

Śāntākāraṃ Analysis

Meghavarṇaṃ: Dark like clouds, full of compassion.

The Śrīvatsa Mark

The mark on the chest from Bhṛgu Maharṣi’s kick—symbolizing the Lord's infinite patience.

Kṛṣṇa & Satyabhāmā

Mediation through the Mother (Lakṣmī) ensures the devotee is accepted despite sins.

Remedies

Chant Śloka 65 for financial debt and liberation from the cycle of saṃsāra.
