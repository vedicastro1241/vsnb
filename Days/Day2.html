Day 2: Pūrvāṅgam: Vighna-Nivāraṇa

The Three Guṇas & Vedic Structure

Human nature is governed by three guṇas:

  • Sattvaguṇa: Clarity and peace.
  • Rajoguṇa: Passion and restlessness.
  • Tamoguṇa: Inertia and resistance.

The Itihāsas: Practical Proof

The ṛṣis composed Purāṇas and Itihāsas to demonstrate Vedic principles.

The Sahasranāmam was given by Bhīṣma to Yudhiṣṭhira.
